🌡️ Measuring Maximum Temperature & Wind Prediction 🌡️

🌍 What is Maximum Temperature?

Maximum temperature is the highest temperature recorded in a day. It is an important meteorological parameter affecting weather, agriculture, and climate studies.

📏 How is Maximum Temperature Measured?

🌞 Mercury Thermometer

Mercury expands as temperature rises, recording the highest temperature reached.

🌡️ Digital Sensors

Electronic sensors in weather stations provide precise temperature readings.

📡 Satellite Imaging

Infrared sensors in satellites measure temperature across the Earth's surface.

🌪️ Why is Maximum Temperature Important for Wind Speed Prediction?

🔥 Heat & Air Pressure

High temperatures cause air to rise, leading to pressure differences that affect wind speed.

🌬️ Wind Formation

Temperature gradients drive wind formation, impacting weather patterns and storms.

📊 Climate Modeling

Long-term temperature data helps predict wind patterns and climate changes.
