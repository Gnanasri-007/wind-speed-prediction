🌡️ Measuring Minimum Temperature 🌡️

❄️ What is Minimum Temperature?

Minimum temperature is the lowest temperature recorded in a day. It plays a key role in climate studies, frost prediction, and environmental monitoring.

📏 How is Minimum Temperature Measured?

🌡️ Alcohol Thermometer

Unlike mercury thermometers, alcohol thermometers are used for very low temperatures because alcohol does not freeze easily.

🌎 Digital Weather Stations

Electronic sensors provide real-time monitoring of minimum temperature for weather forecasting.

📡 Satellites & Infrared Imaging

Advanced climate models use infrared sensors in satellites to detect surface temperature variations.

❄️ Why is Measuring Minimum Temperature Important?

🌨️ Frost & Agriculture

Farmers monitor minimum temperatures to prevent crop damage from frost.

🏔️ Climate & Ecosystem Studies

Understanding temperature variations helps in studying global warming and wildlife adaptations.

🚨 Cold Wave Warnings

Monitoring low temperatures is crucial for issuing alerts during extreme cold weather events.
